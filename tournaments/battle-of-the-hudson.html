About the Tournament

Welcome to the 2nd Annual Battle of the Hudson! This warmup tournament brings together high school teams from New York and New Jersey for a day of competitive ultimate before the spring season kicks off.

Last year's inaugural event was a great success, and we're excited to host again in Brooklyn's Red Hook neighborhood. It's the perfect opportunity to shake off the rust, try out new plays, and build team chemistry before the regular season begins.

Registration is now open!

Tournament Details

Divisions & Pool Structure

  • • Boys/Bx Division: Up to 12 teams across 3 pools (Pool 1 & 2 for A teams, Pool 3 for B teams)
  • • Girls/Gx Division: Up to 4 teams in 1 pool

Schedule

  • • AM Session: Games at 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM
  • • PM Session: Games at 1:00 PM and 3:00 PM
  • • Games Per Team: Each team plays 2 games

Format & Concept

  • • Pre-Season Warmup: Get game reps before the regular season starts
  • • NY vs NJ: Teams from both states compete across the Hudson
  • • High School Teams: Team tournament format (not a hat tournament)

Location

Red Hook Recreation Area

Soccer Fields 4 and 5, Red Hook, Brooklyn, NY

Field Amenities:

  • New turf fields
  • Bathrooms on site
  • Athletic trainer available
  • Water access
