About the Tournament

The Hucksgiving Showdown is a single-day straight to bracket high school team-based ultimate tournament at the Parade Grounds in Brooklyn, NY. This competitive tournament brings together the best youth ultimate teams from the region for an exciting day of high-level play.

With separate boys and girls divisions, all teams are guaranteed 3 games regardless of outcome, ensuring maximum playing time and competitive experience. Limited to 8 teams per division, registration is on a first-come, first-served rolling admission basis.

Tournament Details

Tournament Format

  • • Single-day straight to bracket: Competitive bracket play from the start
  • • 3 games guaranteed: All teams play at least 3 games, win or lose
  • • Capacity: Up to 8 teams per division
  • • Schedule: First round starts at 9am, final round ends by 4pm (exact schedule TBD)
