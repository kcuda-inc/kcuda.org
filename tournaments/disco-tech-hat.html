About the Tournament

Join us for the 4th Annual Brooklyn Disco Tech Support Hat Tournament! This is a fun, inclusive day of ultimate frisbee where teams are formed by picking names from a hat. It's a great opportunity to meet other youth players from NYC and nearby communities while enjoying the spirit of the game.

In a hat tournament, we randomly assign players to teams to ensure fair, balanced games. This format emphasizes teamwork, sportsmanship, and making new friends across the ultimate community.

Tournament Details

Format

  • • Hat Tournament: Teams randomly assigned by drawing names from a hat
  • • Game Size: Small-sided format (4v4 or 5v5)
  • • Mixed Gender: All teams will be mixed gender

Eligibility & Requirements

  • • Age Group: Open to any youth player in 7th grade through 12th grade
  • • USA Ultimate Membership: Required for all participants
  • • Registration: Advance registration required (see registration section below)

Cost & Payment

  • • Entry Fee: $10 per player
  • • Payment: Cash or digital payment accepted on the day of the event
